More About Eagle Construction

Founded in 2011, Eagle Construction is a 100% Basotho-owned building and civil engineering construction business
providing a reliable long-term solution to complex construction challenges to a range of clients since its inception.

We specialize in various expertise spanning well into the construction industry spectrum, from medium renovations to
large projects and infrastructure projects which include water and waste management plants.

With more clients turning to us for our distinctive ability to implement innovative project management techniques,
our success is driven by building the most advanced facilities for our corporate, institutional, and governmental clients.

Services

Eagle Construction offers a range of solutions to any construction-related problems. Whether restoration, site preparation, a new facility, or facility renovation, we have both the experience and the personnel to provide the highest quality construction on schedule and within budget. We execute meticulously, taking extra care with each project.


Project Planning and Design

Eagle Construction provides planning, design, and construction management that covers all the construction processes, architectural plans, site plans, building department approvals, construction, final inspections, and scheduled site meetings.


Construction

We offer construction of facilities; hospitals, schools, offices, water tanks, plumbing, and water treatment plants.


Renovations and Maintenance

We offer the maintenance of old, damaged existing structures.


Industrial Roofing

We construct and roof new structures and also repair damaged roofing.


Concrete Construction

We offer a wide range of concrete construction products including turn-key footings, slabs, and tilt-wall construction, and a supply of concrete products such as concrete blocks.


Electrical Works

At Eagle, we offer a diverse range of electrical work services. From connecting and disconnecting electricity supply wiring to installing, supplying, replacing and repairing electrical equipment.


Civil Works

From small-scale to large-scale projects, Eagle has experience in working on a wide range of civil works projects


Water Reticulation

The catchment of water from the source (i.e. springs), then storing the water in large tanks before it is distributed to consumers. Eagle Construction does gravity and pumping systems, drilling of boreholes and installation of electrical and solar-powered borehole pumps.
